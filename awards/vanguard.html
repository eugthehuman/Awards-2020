Vanguard Awards

Student Category

Groundbreaker
Ésthetique
Risk-taker
People's Choice (voted)

The WKWSCI Vanguard Awards has 4 sub-categories that recognise the creativity, effort and talent of WKWSCI students through their works.

The Groundbreaker honours students who are pioneers or innovate in a particular field of interest.

The Esthétique recognises students who created works with significant aesthetic appeal and a lasting impact on their audience.

The Risk-taker celebrates students who dare to put themselves in precarious positions to cover controversial issues and challenge the status-quo.

Lastly, the People’s Choice is the top voted submission among all other submissions for the Vanguard sub-categories.
